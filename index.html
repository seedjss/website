About S.E.E.D

Established in year 2016 this society aims at exploring and implementing new generation technology and remain in pace with most trending Industrial required skills.

A team of around 40+ members which works together in developing new ideas and technology in fields exclusive to Embedded systems,IoT and AI.We believe to utilize our imagination and work with passion to bring out the best an Engineering student can as young innovators.

Androcar

About

The prima Flagship event of our society with record of highest number of participation in the history of college.Based on developing basics of embedded systems and hands-on Android development among students,it involves building of remote controlled car whose remote is a self build Android App and would communicate wirelessly via bluetooth.All the hardware interfacing and circuitary connection involves self buildup after receiving guidelines and resources in the Workshop.

Androcar Workshop

Androcar Workshop event is organised in month of August-September.Its a 4-5 days event which would constitute learning and developing your own Androcar.All the necessary kits and hardware would be supplied through society by paying the workshop's fee.

Zealicon Prizes

The annual techno-cultural fest of college has this event always on the top of list with prizes worth Rs. 5000 for the winners.

R.C Plane

About R.C Plane

As the name says,its a remote controlled plane in which the plane design,working and mode of execution and other important flight parameters are the area of interest.This deals with the field of Aerodynamics which is the most recent added area of work interest being added by our society to our college's innovation bucket list.

R.C Plane Workshop

This is 5 day Workshop which is held in month of February March.All the resources and materials are supplied by society and the participants are guided by experienced mentors with focus on important factors for successful flight.

Zealicon Prizes

The annual techno-cultural fest hosts the only on-the-air RC plane competition which is very exciting with different challenges and hurdles.The events has a prize money worth Rs. 4000

...and even more

Home Automation

Focusing in the fields ofIoT, Automation and AI,the events aims in building and understanding of Smart home and its controllibity via CLOUD and self-made commands on voice assistants such as Google home,Alexa etc.The workshop is held in month of October-November.

Criminal Minds

A fun Event in which clues are placed all over the campus.Participants have to search for around 10 clues.Each clue leads to another and finally the team finishing with minimum time wins the event.Participants can participate with a team of 4 individuals.Prize money in Zealicon is of worth Rs. 3000

Minute to Win It

A Fun event in which participants are required to complete certain tasks within a minute.There are various task like putting maximum number of tennis balls in a cup in less than a minute etc. The participant who completes all the task in minimum time is declared as winner.Prize money in Zealicon is of worth Rs. 3000
